The Smoked Negroni

timer 5m local_fire_department Med

Artisanal Smoke & Citrus

A sophisticated twist on the classic Italian aperitivo, featuring cold-smoked botanical gin and charred orange essences.

Curator's Note

"The key to this cocktail isn't just the ingredients, but the temperature. We treat the ice as a structural element—each cube carved to maximize surface area without dilution."

Julian Vane

Master Mixologist

format_list_bulleted Ingredients

  • Smoked Botanical Gin 30ml
  • Sweet Red Vermouth 30ml
  • Campari Bitter 30ml
  • Charred Orange Peel 1pc
play_arrow
Watch Technique

01. Preparation Ritual

1

Chill the Vessel

Place your rocks glass in the freezer for at least 15 minutes. A frosted glass is essential for maintaining the textural integrity of the spirit.

2

Combine & Stir

In a mixing glass filled with large-format ice, combine the gin, vermouth, and Campari. Stir gently for exactly 30 seconds to achieve perfect dilution.

3

The Smoke Infusion

Strain the mixture into your chilled glass over a single clear ice block. If using a smoker, trap the wood-smoke under a cloche for 10 seconds before serving.

4

Aromatic Finish

Express the oils of a fresh orange peel over the surface and rub the rim of the glass before dropping it in as a garnish.
